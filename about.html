About AuraLink

AuraLink Technologies designs and builds intelligent radio systems that bridge software-defined radio (SDR), AI-driven signal processing, and next-generation connectivity including V2X and NTN. Our work spans proof-of-concept prototypes, deployable applications, and collaborative R&D.

What we believe
  • Engineering-first: measurable performance, reliable integration, and operational robustness.
  • Openness: built on GNU Radio and industry-standard SDRs (USRP, HackRF, BladeRF).
  • Partnership: we co-create with industry, research labs, and startups.
Domains
  • SDR applications (FM, VHF Marine, Spectrum, Drone detection)
  • Vehicular communications (V2X, NR-V2X Mode 2 prototyping)
  • AI/ML for RF: detection, classification, and adaptive scheduling
Fast facts
  • Founded: 2025
  • Headquarters: Daegu, South Korea
  • Platforms: GNU Radio 3.10+, UHD/USRP, HackRF, BladeRF
  • Engagement: fixed-price projects & research collaborations
